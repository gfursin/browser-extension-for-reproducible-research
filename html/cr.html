Collective Knowledge: find or share knowledge about research papers, code, portable workflows and reusable artifacts on a given web page
When you enable this add-on, only allowed research URLs from your browser tab will be anonymously sent to the open cKnowledge.io portal to retrieve related research papers, state-of-the-art scoreboards, portable workflows and reusable artifacts (see the list of allowed URLs). Note that we do not collect, use, store, and share or disclose information about people!
